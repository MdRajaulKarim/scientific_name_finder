Our Story

Born from a Grandmother's Recipe

ZestyBite started in 2009 as a small kitchen in Bengaluru, driven by a passion for preserving the soul of Indian cooking. Our founder, Chef Priya Mehta, built this restaurant around her grandmother's handwritten recipe book — a treasure of regional flavours passed down through generations.

Today, every dish we serve carries that same spirit: slow-cooked, thoughtfully spiced, and served with warmth.
